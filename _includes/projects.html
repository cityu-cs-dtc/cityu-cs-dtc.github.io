Research Projects

Can LLMs Refuse Questions They Don't Know?

LLM Safety & Alignment

We build a benchmark and metrics to measure whether large language models can recognize the limits of their own knowledge and reliably refuse to answer factual questions they cannot know.

M★: A Memory Harness for LLM Agents

LLM Agents

M★ gives every task its own structured memory harness, letting LLM agents build, retrieve, and reuse task-specific experience for more reliable long-horizon reasoning and tool use.

LMCleaner: Certified Online Unlearning

Machine Unlearning

LMCleaner removes the influence of specific training data from large models efficiently and with certification by truncating influence propagation — no costly full retraining required.

Dual-branch Robust Unlearnable Examples

Unlearnable Examples

We craft unlearnable examples that stop unauthorized models from learning useful features on protected data, while staying robust against adversarial training and data purification.

P²RAG: Privacy-Preserving RAG

Privacy-Preserving AI

P²RAG is an efficient privacy-preserving retrieval-augmented generation service that supports arbitrary top-k retrieval without revealing user queries or the underlying knowledge base.

GasLiteAA: Secure Gas Sponsorship for ERC-4337

Blockchain Systems

GasLiteAA optimizes ERC-4337 account abstraction to enable efficient and secure gas sponsorship, cutting on-chain overhead to make blockchain transactions cheaper and more user-friendly.
